The iSolace Story

Once upon a time... Facebook told me that a friend had passed away. My friend had been suffering from an agressive cancer for several years so the news wasn't fully unexpected but still a shock.

Over the next few days my friend's "wall" turned into a digital memorial.

First, the hours to say a final farewell, later a collage of photos of his life together with a text he had chosen before and finally a thank you for all the well-wishers appeared over several days.

Even though or maybe especially because I was outside of the country at that time the whole "ritual" really touched me. People who had studied with him abroad, old work colleagues, high school friends...

We all left comments/ condolences and achieved closure in a way...
