Frequently asked questions

What is iSolace?

iSolace is a secure free application that will help you to plan ahead by making it easy to prepare and pass on important messages and (digital) assets to your loved ones, family and friends in the event of your passing. It also helps you to get all your ICE information to First Responders including drug allergies. AMD, blood type and more.

Why use iSolace?

Have you considered what will happen to your many online accounts, how you want your assets distributed, who should take care of your dog, your funeral arrangement,... iSolace helps you convey your wishes to Trusted Person(s) of your choice.

How does it work?

With “Contacts and Messages” you can import contacts and prepare personal messages for your loved ones, family and friends should you pass away. Afterward in “My Profile” you can designate up to 3 “Trusted Persons” from the imported contacts to sent important instructions to regarding (1) managing or closing down your online accounts, (2) manage your funeral arrangements (3) convey how you would like your assets to be distributed and (4) communicate the location of your will. We have tried to make this as easy as possible by providing drop-down menus, templates and the option to attach photos/attachments.

Who can be a Trust Person?

You can choose up to 3 Trusted Persons (ideally your next of kin or very close friends) to diligently fulfill your wishes on your funeral arrangements, online accounts, and distribution of your assets. You can also allocate different tasks to different Trusted Persons.

How will the app know when to send my messages?

You can select “Check-In” and “Reminder” periods during which you will be required to log into the application to confirm your well being. If there is no response, then depending on your profile selection: (1) emails will be sent to your Trusted Persons for verification of your passing, before all remaining messages are sent, or (2) all of your emails will be sent without any verification from your selected Trusted Person(s).

How do I ensure my instructions to a Trusted Person(s) are carried out diligently?

The app makes it easy for you to create a list of your possessions and to allocate those fully or percentage wise to your beneficiaries but is NOT legally binding. Soon we will implement the feature to make an actual will in-app and/or to connect you with a local law firm if you have a more complex situation.

Will my information be secure?

Your security and privacy are extremely important to us. The information you store for contacts, contents of messages and assets, plus any attachments are masked and not visible to our iSolace administrators. Any emails that are sent to your contacts are not stored on our email servers.

Where is my data stored?

Your data is stored with Amazon S3 which is a scalable and reliable cloud storage platform, based in Singapore and subject to the Personal Data Protection Act. Your data is securely uploaded/downloaded to Amazon S3 via SSL endpoints using the HTTPS protocol.

How many contacts and messages can I store?

For now you store as many contacts, messages, asset details, plus any attachments that you can fit within 20Mb of storage space (a photo is around 1Mb, a pdf between 0.1 to 2Mb). We will review the capacity to provide more space in the future. We recommend that you save large attachments in established tools such as Dropbox or YouTube and paste the link into your message to maximise the number of messages and assets that you can create.

Who is responsible for the content of messages?

iSolace is a tool to help you pass on messages. By agreeing to our Terms & Conditions, you are responsible for the content of your messages and ensuring it does not violate any laws or regulations.
